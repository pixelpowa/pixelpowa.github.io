Kid Tripp

After two years of development, this fast-paced and tough-as-nails platformer is finally available on the App Store! Have you got what it takes to help Kid Tripp escape from an island filled with enraged wildlife, abandoned mines, bottomless chasms, and erupting volcanos? Many have died trying.
